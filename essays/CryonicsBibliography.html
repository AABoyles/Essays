This follows my self-education in Cryonics, specifically as I would have ordered these materials if I were to start from the beginning again. This is roughly ordered by technical difficulty: the less technical books are at the top, and more technical works follow at the bottom. At the end, I’ve also included a section of books I simply wouldn’t bother with at all, along with my notes about why they fall here.

If you only read one thing…

  • Read the Wait but Why post, Why Cryonics Makes Sense.

Why Should We Consider Cryonics?

Because living is awesome, of course!

  • The Open Letter on Cryonics - There are a bunch of really smart people who openly affirm that Cryonics is a viable avenue to life extension.

  • Robin Hanson - We Agree: Get Froze!

  • Eliezer Yudkowsky - You Only Live Twice

Will it Work?

Probably. However, whether any specific cryonically-preserved individual will be recovered is far less certain.

How do I sign up?

As far as I can tell, there are three agencies world-wide doing cryopreservation. They are:

  • Cryonics Institute
  • Alcor Life Extension Institution
  • KrioRus

Paying for it

  • Life Insurance. Contact Rudi Hoffman.
  • Cash. If you’re pretty wealthy.
