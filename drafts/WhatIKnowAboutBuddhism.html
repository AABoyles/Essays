I have a very close friend who has, over the last few years, come to self-identify as Buddhist. Independently, I have long intended to take up the practice of meditation. It seems like it might have some desirable effects. (Note that I’m not coyly understating my confidence in the efficacy of meditation: I lack sufficient confidence to make such bets.)

In response to Scott Alexander’s book review on Buddhism, I’ve decided to start my own investigation of it as a means for self-improvement.

I intend to write this in multiple passes

Pass 1: I know nothing.

History

I know effectively nothing about the history or traditions of Buddhism.

Meditation Expectations

Pass 2: I know little.
